About Us

A story unfolds in every flavor

Experience the rich and authentic flavors of South India at The Light House. From aromatic filter coffee to crispy dosas, spicy Chettinad curries, and traditional banana leaf meals, we bring you the true essence of South Indian cuisine. Each dish is crafted with fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes, ensuring a feast that delights your taste buds. Join us for a culinary journey that celebrates tradition, taste, and hospitality.
